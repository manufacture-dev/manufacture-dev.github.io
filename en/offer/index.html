_Our coaching adapts to the needs of each team

With several levels of support and intervention formats

Team coaching

Learning Hours

Learn and implement new practices through training (kata, etc.)

Mob Programming (whole or part team)

Develop a backlog functionality by applying the best practices seen during the learning hours

Individual coaching

Pair Programming

Solve problems encountered during Mob sessions or to deepen specific practices

With a focus on people to gain autonomy

To promote acceptable and sustainable skills development, we alternate:

“in” cycles: where the coach is immersed in the team

“out” cycles: where the coach temporarily fades away

“IN” CYCLE

The technical coach accompanies the team in a coaching session (collective or individual)

“OUT” CYCLE

The technical coach lets the team experiment for themselves

The cycles follow the rhythm of the team (on 1 or 2 sprints depending on the duration or on the Program Increment if it exists).

At each start of the immersion cycle, the coach and the team agree on a coaching contract which will define the scope of intervention and the objectives to be achieved.

Note that although it has an impact on team delivery, the technical coach does not take development tasks individually.
